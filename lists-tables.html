Lists

Lists are a great way to organize content. HTML provides:

  • Ordered Lists: Use <ol> for numbered items.
  • Unordered Lists: Use <ul> for points.
  • Nested Lists: Combine <ul> and <ol> for complex structures:
    • Mammals
      • Gorila
      • Mouse
      • Kangaroo
    • Birds
      • Crow
      • Eagle
